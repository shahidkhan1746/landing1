India Travel plan

Trip Description: A 6 Nights 7 Days India tour that helps you explore all the celebrated destinations of India.

Trip Duration: 6 Nights and 7 Days.

Trip Inclusions: Hotel Stay, Breakfast and Dinner & Sightseeing

Places Covered: Dal Lake, Gulmarg, Jaipur, Agra (Taj Mahal), Delhi.

Trip Accommodation: Deluxe Houseboat, Deluxe Hotel

View more details

  • Tour Day 1 - Arrival Srinagar

    After you land in Delhi take a connecting flight to Srinagar. Upon arrival in Srinagar, meet and greet by our company representative/Guide and transfer to deluxe Houseboat/Hotel. After check-in proceed towards shikara ride in the world famous Dal Lake.

  • Tour Day 2 - Srinagar Local Sightseeing

    After breakfast at hotel/houseboat in Srinagar proceed to visit Mughal Gardens built in 17th century by the Mughals. Later proceed to visit the old city of Srinagar.

  • Tour Day 3 - Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar

    After breakfast at Hotel/houseboat in Srinagar proceed towards Gulmarg day trip. Gulmarg is very popular ski resort of India. One can hire cable cars and reach the summit of 14,000 ft above sea level.

  • Tour Day 4 - Srinagar - Delhi

    Catch an early morning flight to Delhi and proceed for a half day sightseeing tour of Delhi. Visit Qutub Minar, Jantar Mantar, Akshardham Temple. Overnight stay in Delhi

  • Tour Day 5 - Delhi - Agra

    After your breakfast at hotel in Delhi proceed towards Agra which is a 3 hour drive from Delhi. After check-in at hotel in Agra, visit the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Overnight stay at hotel in Agra.

  • Tour Day 6 - Agra - Jaipur

    After your breakfast at hotel in Agra proceed towards Jaipur which is a 4 hour drive. Check-in to your hotel in Jaipur and later visit the Amber Fort, a beautiful fort located on a hill overlooking Jaipur. Take an elephant ride up to the Amber Fort. Visit the Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds.

  • Tour Day 7 - Jaipur - Delhi

    After your breakfast at hotel in Jaipur proceed towards delhi to catch a light for next destination. Tour Ends
